Love is a risk. As we pursue it, embrace it, or leave it behind, we’re constantly putting ourselves in a vulnerable position.

This project aims to acknowledge the pains individuals went through, sacrifices they made, effort they put into their “failed” relationships through the form of storytelling. As we build up a collective voices of survivors’ stories, we hope to engage the storytellers and the audiences to look at and interpret these stories, to learn from it, to heal from it, also to grow from it.
